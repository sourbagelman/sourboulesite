Fresh Sourdough Bread

The bread that started it all

Fresh sourdough bread is made and baked daily at our Fort Worth bakery, then delivered each morning for maximum freshness.

Our carefully maintained starter gives every loaf its own character, flavor, and texture.

Available by the loaf: Plain, Rosemary Garlic, and Jalapeño Cheddar.
