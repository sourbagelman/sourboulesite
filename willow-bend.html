Lunch + dinner rhythm

Planned hours are currently 10:30 AM – 9:00 PM, built around lunch, dinner, and pickup traffic.

Service Style

Built for pickup

This location is being designed primarily for to-go ordering, quick pickup, and food that travels well.

Menu Direction

Sourdough comfort food

Expect burgers, sandwiches, bagel builds, smoked meats, sides, drinks, and specials shaped around the Sour Boule style.

Same DNA

Not a different restaurant

Willow Bend is not meant to replace the Fort Worth café. It is a different service model built from the same core ideas: slow bread, house-made food, and real ingredients.

The goal is simple: make The Sour Boule easier to enjoy later in the day.
