Now Growing

Two rhythms. One Sour Boule.

The Sour Boule started with bread.

Real sourdough. Slow fermentation. Ingredients that actually matter.

That foundation built the Fort Worth café — a breakfast and lunch spot where mornings move fast, sandwiches stack high, and the bread still does most of the talking.

Now the story is expanding.

Willow Bend brings a different rhythm: lunch, dinner, pickup, burgers, hit dogs, salads, sandwiches, and comfort food built to travel.

It is not a different restaurant.

It is the same idea in a new format.

Slow bread.

Real ingredients.

Food made with intention.

Just later in the day.

Fort Worth remains the original café. Willow Bend becomes the pickup-focused lunch and dinner kitchen.
