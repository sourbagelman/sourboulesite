Private Events

Host something unique at The Sour Boule

Small gatherings, workshops, and creative events can sometimes take place at the café. Most events happen outside normal business hours, but we can occasionally work around operating hours depending on the event and space needs.

Workshops

Classes and maker events

Craft nights, teaching sessions, and small workshops work well in the café setting. If you host recurring creative events or small classes, the space can often be arranged to fit your setup.

Private Gatherings

Celebrations and meetups

Birthdays, small celebrations, community meetups, and casual gatherings can work well in the space depending on group size and timing.

Creative Events

Something different

Book clubs, tastings, networking groups, and other unique ideas are always worth asking about. The best events are usually the ones that bring people together around something interesting.

Good to know

Event basics

  • Most events take place outside normal business hours
  • Some events may work during open hours depending on size
  • Setup time is usually available before the event
  • Each event is reviewed individually
  • Outside food and drinks may be allowed depending on the event
